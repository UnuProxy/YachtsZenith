Discover the allure of Ibiza with Yachts Zenith. Cruise along the island's stunning coastline, uncovering hidden coves and pristine beaches. Whether you seek adventurous water sports, tranquil sailing, or vibrant nightlife, our diverse fleet promises an unforgettable journey through the Balearics.

Explore our luxury yacht rentals in Ibiza
Experience the laid-back charm of Ibiza while exploring secluded coves, indulging in local cuisine, and basking in the Mediterranean sun.

Discover Ibiza's Finest Dining

Embark on a culinary journey with these top restaurant picks:

  • Lio: Enjoy live shows and stunning views at this famous spot in Marina Botafoch.
  • Cipriani: Taste top-notch Italian food in Marina Ibiza's elegant setting.
  • Amante Beach Club: Experience great Mediterranean food and amazing views above Sol d'en Serra beach.
  • Cala Bonita: Try authentic Spanish dishes right by the beach.

Must-See Attractions for Yacht Charters in Ibiza

Discover Ibiza's beauty and history:

  • Old Town (Dalt Vila): Explore streets recognised by UNESCO and see the famous Santa Maria Cathedral and Ibiza Castle.
  • Es Vedrà: Be amazed by the magical view of this famous rock from your yacht.
  • Formentera: Visit this beautiful spot for its clear beaches and quaint villages.
  • Cala Comte: Enjoy relaxing at one of Ibiza's prettiest beaches, known for its clear turquoise waters and stunning views.

Popular Activities for Yacht Visitors in Ibiza

Make your Ibiza visit unforgettable with these fun activities:

  • Aquatic Sports: Enjoy the clear waters! Try jet-skiing, paddleboarding, or snorkelling.
  • Beach Clubs: Relax at famous spots like Ushuaia, Nikki Beach, and Blue Marlin.
  • Sunset Cruises: See Ibiza's famous sunsets from your yacht at Café del Mar or Es Vedrà.
  • Yoga and Wellness: Refresh yourself with yoga or spa treatments in peaceful spots.

Ibiza's allure extends beyond its beautiful beaches and vibrant nightlife. Contact us to tailor your yacht charter experience and create lifelong memories in this enchanting destination.

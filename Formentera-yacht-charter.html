FAQs

What is the best time to visit Formentera?

The ideal time for a yacht charter in Formentera is between May and November, offering warm temperatures and pleasant sailing conditions.

Do I need a boating license to charter a yacht?

In most cases, a boating license is not required for yacht charters that come with a skipper. However, for bareboat charters, at least one person onboard must have a valid boating license.

What is included in a yacht charter?

Our yacht charters typically include the yacht, a professional crew, and necessary safety equipment. Additional services like meals, fuel, and marina fees may vary depending on the package.

Can I customise my itinerary?

Absolutely! We encourage you to work with our team to create a personalized itinerary that suits your interests and preferences.

What should I bring on my yacht charter?

We recommend bringing sunscreen, hats, sunglasses, comfortable clothing, swimwear, and any personal items you may need. Towels and basic amenities are provided onboard.

How far in advance should I book my charter?

To ensure the availability of your preferred yacht and to accommodate any special requests, we recommend booking at least 3 months in advance, especially during peak season.

How do we disembark from the yacht to visit the beaches?

Disembarking for beach visits is seamlessly facilitated by our skilled crew using tenders (small boats) to transport guests from the yacht to the shore, ensuring easy access to even the most secluded beaches. For beach landings, we always prioritise safety and comfort, providing guidance and assistance as needed.

Are there any recommended restaurants near popular mooring spots?

Absolutely! Formentera boasts a variety of exquisite dining options that cater to all tastes, ranging from local seafood taverns to upscale Mediterranean cuisine. Near popular mooring spots, we highly recommend 'El Pirata' for its fresh, locally sourced dishes and 'La Savina', known for its stunning seaside views and gourmet menu. Our crew is happy to make reservations and arrange transportation for an unforgettable culinary experience.

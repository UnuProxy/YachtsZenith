Before You Embark

Selecting the Right Yacht: When considering a yacht charter in Ibiza, the choice of vessel is paramount. Reflect on the following:

Number of Passengers: The right boat hire in Ibiza should have enough cabins and space to comfortably accommodate everyone in your party.

Destination: Each destination, whether it's the tranquil waters of Formentera or the iconic Es Vedra, may influence your choice of boat rental in Ibiza. As you plan to hire a yacht in Ibiza, consider the diverse experiences each area offers. The serene beaches of Formentera are perfect for leisurely cruises, while the mystique of Es Vedra calls for more adventurous explorations.

Preferences and Amenities: From water toys to fine dining, identify what services and amenities you'd like included in your Ibiza boat charter.

Duration: For extended yacht charters in Ibiza, comfort and onboard amenities become increasingly important. Opt for yachts that offer luxurious accommodations, spacious decks, and advanced navigation technology for longer stays.

Specific Needs: Any particular requirements, such as accessibility or dietary preferences, should be specified when arranging your boat rental in Ibiza.

For furthe detiled information you can visit Royal Yachting Association website

VAT and Yacht Fuel Consumption

VAT Remember that is applicable to yacht charters in Ibiza. The standard rate is 21%, so it's important to factor this into your overall budget.

APA (Advance Provisioning Allowance): Typically for boat rentals in Ibiza, the base rate doesn't cover fuel. Instead, you'll pay an APA, generally around 20-30% of the charter fee. This allowance is used to estimate and prepay for fuel, provisions, and berthing charges, ensuring a seamless experience on your Ibiza yacht hire.

Fuel Considerations: In most yacht charters in Ibiza, the cost of fuel is not included in the base charter fee. This approach allows for more flexibility and customisation of your journey, whether you're exploring the tranquil waters of Ibiza or venturing to the scenic cliffs of Es Vedra.

For detailed guidelines and safety measures related to yacht chartering in Ibiza, please refer to the Ibiza Maritime Authority website.

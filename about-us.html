Our Voyage

Yachts Zenith has quickly become a prominent name in Ibiza, renowned for its exceptional yacht hire services in the Balearic Islands. Specialising in luxurious and unforgettable yacht experiences, we focus on the natural elegance and laid-back charm of Ibiza and the surrounding Balearic archipelago. Our founder, Julian Pirvu, a passionate sea enthusiast, leads the way in crafting unique voyages that highlight the serene beauty and vibrant culture of these Mediterranean gems. While we have roots in London, our fleet in Ibiza is celebrated for its outstanding quality and discreet service, far surpassing standard boat rentals.

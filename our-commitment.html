Weather-Related Rescheduling

At Yachts Zenith, we understand that weather can be unpredictable, but your exceptional experience should not be. Over the years, we've seamlessly navigated weather-related challenges, ensuring our clients' plans remain as uninterrupted as possible. Here are a few ways we ensure your journey remains on course:

  • Alternative Dates: Should the forecast suggest less than ideal conditions, we offer the flexibility to select alternative dates for your charter, ensuring you sail under the best skies.
  • New Destinations: If your chosen destination is affected by adverse weather, we're ready with recommendations for equally stunning locations unaffected by the weather, allowing you to continue your adventure without compromise.
  • Complimentary Upgrades: In the rare event that changes are needed, we provide complimentary amenities or upgrades where possible, enhancing your experience beyond your original expectations.

These examples underscore our commitment to delivering a seamless and enjoyable yacht charter experience, regardless of the weather. Your safety and satisfaction are our top priorities, and we go above and beyond to ensure that a change in weather simply means a new opportunity for adventure with Yachts Zenith.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is covered by the satisfaction guarantee?

Our satisfaction guarantee covers all aspects of your yacht charter experience, from the condition of the yacht to the quality of service provided. If any part of your charter does not meet your expectations, we commit to addressing your concerns promptly. This may include offering alternative solutions, adjustments to your service, or, in some cases, compensation.

How do I reschedule my charter due to poor weather?

If adverse weather conditions affect your booking, please contact us as soon as possible. We offer flexible rescheduling options to ensure you can enjoy your charter experience at a more suitable time, without any additional cost.

Can I customise my itinerary?

Yes, absolutely. We pride ourselves on offering bespoke yacht charters tailored to your preferences. From selecting your destinations to planning activities, our team is here to create your perfect itinerary. Let us know your wishes, and we'll make it happen.

What safety measures are in place on the yacht?

Your safety is our top priority. Each yacht is equipped with the latest safety equipment and adheres to strict safety protocols. Our crew is professionally trained in emergency procedures to ensure your well-being throughout the journey.

How can I provide feedback on my charter experience?

We welcome your feedback as it helps us improve our services. After your charter, we'll send you a feedback survey via email. Additionally, you can contact us anytime via our website or phone to share your thoughts and suggestions.

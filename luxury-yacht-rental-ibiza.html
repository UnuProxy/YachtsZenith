Luxury and Personalised Service

Welcome to the pinnacle of Ibiza's luxury yacht charters. Here at Yachts Zenith, we reimagine what it means to go on custom trips around the Mediterranean's pristine waterways. Every yacht in our illustrious fleet radiates style, refinement, and unmatched comfort, guaranteeing not just a voyage but an experience crafted to the highest levels of exclusivity and luxury. Our committed concierge service makes sure every aspect of your charter is perfectly performed, from carefully chosen itineraries to fine dining options catered to your preferences, from the first consultation to the last anchor.

Curate Your Dream Voyage with Our Premier Fleet

Take in the finest assortment of luxury yachts, carefully chosen for discriminating voyagers such as yourself. Every vessel is a shining example of luxury, furnished with cutting-edge conveniences and leisure equipment to provide guests with an amazing maritime experience. Whether you're organising a large celebration, a family holiday, or an intimate get-away, let us guide you on an extraordinary journey across the enchanted seas of Ibiza.

Data Policy for Yachts Zenith

Welcome to Yachts Zenith. This Data Policy explains how Yachts Zenith (referred to as "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, shares, and safeguards your personal information when you visit our yacht charter website at www.yachtszenith.com. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Spanish data protection laws.

Information We Collect

We collect various types of information, including: Personal Information, Technical Data, and Usage Data.

How We Collect Your Data

Your data is collected through Direct Interactions: When you fill out forms, subscribe to our services, request information, or contact us directly.

Use of Your Data

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • Personalising and enhancing your experience on our site.
  • Communicating with you, such as responding to your enquiries and sending service-related communications.
  • Improving our website and services.

Data Protection

  • Strict data processing policies and procedures in compliance with GDPR and Spanish data protection laws.
  • Regularly updated security practices and protocols to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction of your data.
  • Encryption of data during transmission and storage.
  • Regular audits and assessments to ensure the effectiveness of our data protection measures.
  • Training and awareness programs for our employees to handle your data securely.

We are committed to continuously improving our data protection strategies to safeguard your personal information against emerging threats and vulnerabilities.

Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data, including encryption, firewalls, and secure server facilities.

Your Rights

Under the GDPR and Spanish data protection laws, you have rights concerning your personal data, including the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, and data portability.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Data Policy periodically. Any changes will be communicated through our website, with the date of the latest update indicated at the top of the policy.

Contact Us

For any queries regarding this Data Policy or your personal data, please contact us at info@yachtszenith.com.

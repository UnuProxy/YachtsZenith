Discovering Lady Lara: The Epitome of Luxury at Sea

By Yachts Zenith, 26.02.2024

Key Specifications

Lady Lara, a majestic 91-metre superyacht, represents the pinnacle of luxury yachting, designed by the esteemed Reymond Langton Design and delivered by the prestigious German shipyard Lürssen in 2015. With accommodation for 18 guests and a crew of 40, she offers unparalleled luxury and service.

Interior Elegance

The interior of Lady Lara, designed by Reymond Langton Design, is a testament to luxury and comfort, accommodating 18 guests in lavish style. Featuring VIP suites with fold-down balconies, a bright and welcoming ambiance, and extensive health and beauty centres including a spa and beach club, Lady Lara offers an opulent yachting experience.

Exterior Design

With an exterior also crafted by Reymond Langton Design, Lady Lara features sweeping curves and a harmonious balance of elegance and strength. Her full displacement hull and sophisticated lighting create a stunning visual display, highlighting her white aluminium superstructure and teak decks.

Advanced Specifications

Lady Lara boasts advanced specifications including a maximum speed of 18 knots and a cruising speed of 14 knots, powered by twin MTU engines. Her sophisticated stabilisation system ensures utmost comfort at sea. Classified by Lloyd's Register, she adheres to the highest standards of safety and performance.

  • Length Overall (LOA): 91 meters (298 feet)
  • Beam: 14.8 meters (48.5 feet)
  • Draught: 4.2 meters (13.8 feet)
  • Gross Tonnage: Approximately 2,950 GT
  • Year Built: 2015
  • Builder: Lürssen Yachts
  • Naval Architect: Lürssen Yachts
  • Exterior Design: Reymond Langton Design
  • Interior Design: Reymond Langton Design
  • Accommodation: Up to 12 guests in 6 staterooms, including a master suite, 3 VIP staterooms, and 2 twin cabins. Crew accommodations for up to 29 members.
  • Engines: 2 x Caterpillar (12V 4000 M73) producing 2,984hp each
  • Maximum Speed: 18 knots
  • Cruising Speed: 12 knots
  • Range: 4,000 nautical miles at cruising speed

Amenities and Features

Lady Lara is outfitted with an array of luxurious amenities and bespoke features for the utmost comfort and entertainment of her guests:

  • Expansive beach club with fold-down terraces
  • On-deck Jacuzzi and swimming pool
  • Fully equipped gym and spa
  • Cinema room
  • Helipad for easy access
  • State-of-the-art stabilization system for smooth sailing
  • Advanced navigation and communication systems

Sustainability and Innovation

In line with modern yachting standards, Lady Lara incorporates several sustainable practices and innovations to minimize her environmental impact:

  • Energy-efficient propulsion system
  • Advanced wastewater treatment facilities
  • Use of eco-friendly materials in construction and interiors

Award-Winning Recognition

Awarded at the Asia Boating Awards in 2016, Lady Lara is not just a testament to luxury yachting but also to nautical innovation, showcasing cutting-edge design and engineering that places her at the forefront of the maritime world.
